📖 Tournament Rules

  1. Competition will be played using a league format.
  2. Group winners advance to the final, while second-placed teams compete for third place.
  3. Drawn matches during the group stage remain draws and earn 1 point each.
  4. Tiebreakers: Goal Difference → Goals For → Head-to-Head → Penalty Shootout.
  5. No favoritism will be tolerated during the competition.
  6. Matches will be played 6-a-side with up to 4 substitutes.
  7. Each match consists of two 30-minute halves with a 5-minute halftime break.
  8. The offside rule will not apply.
  9. Maximum of 4 substitutions per game.
  10. Late arrival results in a one-goal penalty. More than 20 minutes late results in a 0–3 forfeiture.
  11. Red card fine: ₦500.
  12. Yellow card fine: ₦300.
  13. Points system: Win = 3, Draw = 1, Loss = 0.
  14. Players are encouraged to wear shin guards and proper equipment.
  15. Violent conduct may lead to team disqualification.
  16. Referees' and organizers' decisions are final.
